EB-5: What and Why

The EB-5 Immigrant Investor Visa is a US federal program that allows qualified foreign investors and their immediate family members to obtain their permanent residency (green card) by making an investment in the US that creates jobs.

In the last 5 years, over $40 billion has been invested in the US by foreign nationals applying for the EB-5 Investor Visa. The program is well established and has been renewed over 30 times with bipartisan support in Congress. On average over 90% of applicants are approved by the US government. Over 78,000 people have invested in EB-5 projects to receive their US Green Cards.
